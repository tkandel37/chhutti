    OUR HOTELS

  • Gaia Holiday Home
      • Located in Dhulikhel, 32 km from Kathmandu, Gaia Holiday Home features a barbecue and children's playground. The hotel has a sun terrace and views of the mountain, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. Every room is equipped with a TV with cable channels. Certain rooms feature a seating area where you can relax. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. Both free WiFi and free private parking are available at this property.
  • Hotel Access Nepal
      • Hotel Access Nepal is conveniently located a 5-minute walk from the city centre and provides spacious accommodation with views of the city. The on-site restaurant offers a selection of Indian, European and Chinese dishes.The hotel is about 5 km from Tribhuvan International Airport and a 7-minute drive from Monkey Temple. There is a bus stop, Kantipath Bus Stand, a 5-minute walk away.Hotel Access Nepal’s rooms and suites offer city views and feature a flat-screen TV, personal safe and tea/coffee making facilities.
  • Everest Hotel
      • Hotel Everest Nepal is situated in Thamel, 1 km from Hanuman Dhoka. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV. Certain units feature a seating area or a balcony to relax in after a busy day. Every room includes a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries.There is free shuttle service at the property.
  • Himalayan House Resort
      • Situated in Dhulikhel, OYO 363 Himalayan House Resort offers 2-star accommodation with a bar. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests.A continental breakfast is available each morning at the hotel.Kathmandu is 30 km from OYO 363 Himalayan House Resort, while Nagarkot is 27 km from the property.
  • Fulchoki Shangrila Home
      • Fulchoki Shangrila Home offers accommodation in Godāvari. This 2-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and free WiFi. The hotel has family rooms.All guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. At OYO 389 Fulchoki Shangrila Home all rooms have a private bathroom with a shower.A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.Kathmandu is 14 km from OYO 389 Fulchoki Shangrila Home, while Nagarkot is 36 km from the property.
  • Truly Asia
      • Located in Kathmandu, 1.3 km from Hanuman Dhoka, Truly Asia Boutique Hotel provides accommodation with a restaurant, private parking, a bar and a shared lounge. All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.All units at the hotel include air conditioning and a wardrobe.Guests at Truly Asia Boutique Hotel can enjoy a continental breakfast.
